How To Play

Over 7 rounds, aim for the Target Number without going over.

You will be playing with a 52-card standard deck plus 5 Jokers.

Each round, each player take two cards or pass.

  • Odd rounds add your taken cards to your score.
  • Even rounds subtract them from your score.
  • Draw a Joker, and your score resets to zero.

After all 7 rounds, whoever is closest to the target wins.
